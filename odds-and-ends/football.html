“Home team (away)”:

I happened to be a fan of Cristiano Ronaldo and Real Madrid when I was 11. I only knew that Lionel Messi and Ronaldo were the two best players at that time, so I just picked one by instinct. (That’s why I look forward to reading the book Home Team Away: Watching European Football in Urban China.) Another home team of mine was Guangzhou Evergrande (Guangzhou F.C.) because it was literally my “home” team and it won so many championships back then. I kept up with football trends for about five years. Now I seldom watch football and have no home team, only hoping to enjoy every good match.
